Case Study

Swagga.ai

Paid Brand Campaign

Objective

Introduce Swagga.ai's AI content-generation tool in a way that felt native to TikTok, not like a tech-product ad.

Challenges

AI tools can come across as gimmicky or hard to explain in under a minute, and the video needed to hook viewers before ever mentioning the product.

My Strategy

Opened with a personal, self-deprecating hook, kept the setting casual, and used a quick screen recording to show the actual product before a clear next step.

Outcome

Concept sample led directly to selection for a paid brand campaign, ahead of the portfolio site even being finished.
